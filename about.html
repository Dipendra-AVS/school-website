About Akhanda Vidya Sadan

Established in 1980 AD, Akhanda Vidya Sadan has been serving quality education for over four decades in Kalanki, Kathmandu.

Our Vision

To develop disciplined, mindful, and responsible global citizens.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education
  • Integrate mindfulness in daily learning
  • Develop character and leadership

Principal's Message

Education is not just about academics but about shaping life. We are committed to nurturing the whole child.

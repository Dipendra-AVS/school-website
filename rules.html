Rules and Regulations for Students

Uniform & Grooming

  • Sunday, Monday, Wednesday, Thursday:
  • White Shirt, Gray Pants, Tie, Belt
  • Black Shoes with Gray Socks

  • Tuesday & Friday:
  • House Tracksuit
  • White Shoes and Socks

  • Hair must be neat and short
  • No jewelry, makeup, or long nails

Punctuality & Conduct

  • Students must arrive before 9:15 AM
  • Respect toward teachers and peers is mandatory
  • Mobile phones and gadgets are strictly prohibited
  • Minimum 90% attendance required

Rules and Regulations for Parents

  • Communication: Check school diary daily for updates
  • Montessori Support: Encourage independent learning at home
  • Punctuality: Timely drop-off and pick-up is required
  • Fee Policy: Pay fees before the 7th of every month
  • Health: Do not send sick children to school
Note: Akhanda Vidya Sadan reserves the right to modify these regulations to maintain a safe, disciplined, and effective learning environment.

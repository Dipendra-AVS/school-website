Welcome to Akhanda Vidya Sadan

Established in 1980 AD, Akhanda Vidya Sadan is a premier English Medium Private School located in Kalanki, Kathmandu-14. With over four decades of academic excellence, we offer a comprehensive educational journey from the foundational years to Grade 10. The school is committed to fostering a disciplined yet creative environment where every student can thrive.

School Introduction

At Akhanda Vidya Sadan, we believe that education is more than just textbooks; it is about building character. The school operates from 9:15 AM to 4:00 PM, providing a structured and engaging atmosphere.

Montessori Method is integrated in our pre-primary wing, ensuring hands-on learning, independence, and creativity.

We take pride in our long-standing history in the Kalanki area, fostering a legacy of successful alumni who lead with integrity and knowledge.

Rules and Regulations for Students

Uniform & Grooming

  • Sun, Mon, Wed, Thu: Formal Uniform
  • White Shirt, Gray Pants, Tie, Belt
  • Black Shoes with Gray Socks
  • Tue & Fri: House Tracksuit
  • White Shoes and Socks
  • Hair must be neat and short
  • No jewelry, makeup, or long nails

Punctuality & Conduct

  • Arrival before 9:15 AM is mandatory
  • Respect for teachers and peers
  • Mobile phones strictly prohibited
  • Minimum 90% attendance required

Rules and Regulations for Parents

  • Communication: Check school diary daily
  • Montessori Support: Encourage independent learning at home
  • Punctuality: Timely drop-off and pick-up
  • Fee Policy: Pay before 7th of each month
  • Health: Keep sick children at home
We believe a child’s success is a partnership between home and school.
